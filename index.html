ABOUT US

Street Kala is a social business initiative that provides platform for proficient street artists who make living out of sympathetic money. The enterprise explores for talented artists on city streets and subjects them to a specific training period before unmasking them for public performances. During the entire course the artists would be ensured employment on regular basis.

Through Street Kala customers can hire these artists for various occasions which include marriages, college fests , theatre performances etc , through user interactive application and accordingly pay them for their performances.

We believe in sanctity and cultral integrity by providing appropriate stage for artists that would help revive the Indian folk and also aim for worldwide promotion of the ethnic Indian cultural thorugh International performances at various music and film festivals.
